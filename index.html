A chatbot answers FAQs from a script. Your AI sidekick is trained on your specific business — your products, pricing, tone, and workflows — and actively takes action: booking appointments, capturing leads, sending follow-ups, and routing tasks to your team.

Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

None at all. We handle everything — the build, the integrations, the training, and the ongoing management. You show up to the onboarding call and we take it from there.

What platforms does it connect to?

Your AI sidekick integrates with your website, CRM, calendar, email, SMS, Google Business, Facebook, Instagram, and more. We map out your specific stack during the discovery call.

How long does it take to go live?

Most clients are live within 5 business days of the onboarding call. Complex integrations may take a few days longer, but we'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Is this only for insurance agencies?

No — your AI sidekick works for any small business: trades, real estate, retail, professional services, health and wellness, and more. The agents are custom-built for your industry and business model.

What happens after I sign up?

You'll receive a welcome email with your onboarding call link. In that call, we map your business, design your agent suite, and get everything we need to build. You'll be live within 5 days.
